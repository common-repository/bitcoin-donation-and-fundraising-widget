== Description ==
Accept Bitcoin, Ethereum and other popular cryptocurrencies as donation onto your WordPress website.

Cryptocurrency Donation Widget is a WordPress donation plugin empowering bloggers to receive crypto donations for their online creative content.

Generate simple QR Code within your chosen widget area. The easiest and simplest solution to receive cryptocurrencies for supporting promising projects and fundraising purposes.

Everybody owning and running a WordPress blog can receive Digital Crypto Currencies such as: BTC, ETH, TRON, Monero, Litecoin and more, and be part of the globally growing Blockchain technology trend.

You can track all donations and crypto transactions made to your Bitcoin Wallet Address within bytemart.org centralized bookkeeping database and within bitcoin blockchain explorers.

Donors can donate in the Crypto Coins and Crypto Tokens of their choice:
Supported Crypto coins and tokens: Bitcoin, Bitcoin Cash, Litecoin, BNB Coin (Mainnet), Dash, DigiByte, Dogecoin, Ether, NEO, Qtum, Ravencoin, Stratis, TRON, Tether USD (Omni Layer), Vertcoin, Waves, NEM, Monero, VERGE and many more.

Features:
* Accept donations on your website in various Cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin, Ether or Dogecoin.
* Crypto donations are full refundable to donor within 48 hours if he or she made any mistakes.
* Receive Bitcoin payouts to your Bitcoin wallet address every 48 hours.
* Bitcoin, Ether, XRP, Dash, Digibyte, Monero and other popular cryptocurrencies are accepted.

Who can use Cryptocurrency Donation Widget?
Everybody running a WordPress blog can install this crypto donation widget plugin and receive crypto currency donations.


== Installation ==
1. Upload "bytemart" to the "/wp-content/plugins/" directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress.
3. Add the widget 'Bitcoin Donation and Fundraising Widget' to your chosen widget location.
4. Important! You will have to add your bitcoin Wallet Address within the widget setting.
